This is my second-most recent project. Although it isn't the first WordPress site I've done, it IS the first Ecommerce site I've ever made. I made this site for the wife of one of my good college buddies. Right around late summer of 2020, he had approached me, inquiring about my experience with WordPress and moreover, with ecommerce site. He was wondering as his wife is quite the crafter. Crafting for her is a life-long hobby that she realized she can make extra cash with. With knowledge of the more lucrative side of her hobby, she was interested in exploring ways she could sell her crafts online. Although I hadn't yet created a ecommerce store beofre, I was inspired by her initive and was up for the challenge. The project took about 3 months to complete and is helping her make side cash as we speak!

Being a WordPress site, it was predominatly made up of PHP code (with some HTML, CSS and JavaScript mixed in). This project provided me with a lot of challenges to keep me busy as PHP isn't one of the primary programming lanuagges that I'm afluent in. However, I was up for overcoming the learing curve. During this project, I learned a TON about PHP. More specifically, how to combine HTML and CSS with PHP in order to make custom changes, to theemes and the overall functionality of the WordPress stie. The amount of customizability that I was able to bring to the table, the cleint ended up with a custom product taylored to her specific requests (something you don't get with any one WordPress theme). Not only did I gain a valuable amount of experience doing custom PHP work, I also gained a tremendous amount of exprience learning not only how to integrate an online store using WordPress, but also how to make a WordPress site secure. I learned a TON about security, configuring httaccess files, and finding a healthy balance of plugins.

I would also like to touch on how much exposure I gained as it related to the design ---> development process. Throughout this project, I helped guide my client thorght the creation of a style-guide, as well as applying that style-guide thorughout the duration of the project. Doing this helped me lean how to facilitate success. I'm proud to say that the site is still live and is growing orgaically everyday. My friend and his wife are now realizing the benifits of having a ecommerce site at thier disposal. As I type this paragraph, the site is helping them generate extra income as they are expecting thier second child later this year. Lastly, I'd like to share that up till this point in time, the site has succusessfully blocked 1,500 + malicious attempts to hack the site.
